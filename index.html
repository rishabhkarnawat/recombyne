How it works

From raw conversation to decision-ready intelligence.

Four stages. Every query runs the full pipeline in under two seconds.

1 Ingest

Pull the open web, continuously.

Recombyne ingests posts, comments, and replies from X, Reddit, and a growing set of sources. Every piece of content is timestamped, attributed, and stored the moment it is published.

2 Score

Understand tone, not just keywords.

A transformer-based NLP model scores every post for sentiment polarity and confidence. Slang, sarcasm, abbreviations, and platform-specific language are handled natively.

3 Weight

Measure what actually reaches people.

Every score is multiplied by the engagement it earned — likes, retweets, upvotes, comment volume. A whisper and a viral post are not the same thing. Recombyne knows the difference.

4 Surface

Deliver the signal, not the noise.

Your dashboard delivers sentiment timelines, entity comparisons, divergence alerts, and exportable reports. You see not just what people think, but exactly how much it matters.
